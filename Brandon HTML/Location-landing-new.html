Patient FAQ

Do you accept my health insurance? expand_more
We accept most major commercial insurance plans, Medicare, and several Medicaid Managed Care organizations. Please call us to confirm your specific plan is in-network.
Is same-day appointment availability offered? expand_more
Yes! We reserve several slots daily for acute or urgent care needs. Contact our office early in the morning for the best chance of securing a same-day visit.
How can I access my test results? expand_more
Results are typically available within 48-72 hours via our secure Patient Portal. You will receive an email notification once your clinician has reviewed and released your results.
